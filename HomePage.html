About The Festival

Some interesting facts about the music festival

  • The festival is held at "Downsview Park Toronto, Ontario, Canada".
  • This is a two-day electronic dance music (EDM) festival founded by INK entertainment
  • The festival was first held in 2012. Since then, the popularity of the festival has only grown.
